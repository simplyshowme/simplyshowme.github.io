SimplyShow.me

A video service that allows professionals to share their expertise privately



What is SimplyShow.me?

SimplyShow.me allows a business or professional to engage clients in a compelling, convenient and private fashion. With SimplyShow.me, sharing and monetising your knowledge is as simple as pressing 'Record'.

SimplyShow.me is built as a video messaging service, allowing professionals, teachers and instructors of any discipline to create and share informative video messages with their clients or students, direct to their smart phones. What sets SimplyShow.me apart from other video services is its privacy. Professionals have complete control over who can view their content, allowing them to protect their intellectual property. From the client's perspective, it provides a safe way for them to seek and receive advice from their professional.

Is This For You?

SimplyShow.me is well suited to both small and large business, from sole traders with little technical background looking to further break into the market to multinational corporations seeking a modern and effective means of reaching their customers.

Examples of clients currently using SimplyShow.me include golf instructors, personal trainers and Pilates instructors.
